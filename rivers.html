Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

What makes desolation and Gray Canyon unique in Utah?

  • Based on the uniqueness and rich history that the canyon tells, it has been recognized as a National Landmark.
  • Desolation is rated a Class II/III and is usually done in 6 days.
  • High grey stone cliffs, beautiful desert scenery, and great rapids have made this run famous.
  • This 84-mile trip runs from from Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid.
  • Evidence of prehistoric and historic human activity, and great whitewater opportunities.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

This is the Grand daddy of all canyons and the grand daddy of all river trips. You will never forget your visit to the Grand Canyon for the following reasons:

  • Grand Canyon river rafting combines world-class whitewater with breathtaking scenery
  • The canyon is filled with exhilarating whitewater rapids
  • The canyon also contains side canyons and ancient indian ruins accessible only by river.
